Housing Equity Census / HOLC dbt + Snowflake Python / geopandas
What it is
HOLC redlining grades from the 1930s joined to 2024 rent burden and homeownership data at the census tract level. Does where a neighborhood was graded 90 years ago still predict who can afford to live there today?
Data Sources
Mapping Inequality (Richmond) — HOLC polygons

Policy Actions This Data Supports
  • Identify cities where Grade D tracts have highest rent burden for targeted Community Reinvestment Act enforcement
  • Rank metros by HOLC-homeownership gap to prioritize down payment assistance programs
  • Support reparations research by quantifying cumulative wealth loss in redlined tracts since 1940
  • Flag banks with lending deserts that overlap historically redlined areas for CFPB review
