What it is
BLS Current Population Survey microdata modeled at the worker level. The aggregate gap number gets thrown around constantly. This looks at what's underneath it — how it shifts by industry, occupation, race, gender, and education level.
Data Sources
BLS CPS PUMS — worker-level microdata
