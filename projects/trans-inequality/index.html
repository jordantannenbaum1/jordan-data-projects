The Hidden Crisis:
Transgender Inequality
in America

Trans Equity Lifespan · Income · Housing USTS · CDC · HUD dbt + Snowflake
What it is
A multi-dimensional look at inequality faced by transgender Americans, with specific focus on trans women of color who face compounding disparities in life expectancy, income, housing stability, and violence. The data exists. It just rarely gets modeled together.
Data Sources
US Trans Survey (USTS) — income, housing, employment
CDC NCHHSTP — health outcomes, violence data
Trans Murder Monitoring — homicide rates by demographic
HUD PIT Count — trans homelessness estimates

Policy Actions This Data Supports
  • Identify states where trans homicide rates are highest to make the case for hate crime statute expansion
  • Quantify the shelter gap for trans individuals to support dedicated HUD funding allocations
  • Build an employer equity scorecard using income disparity data to inform state nondiscrimination ordinances
  • Ground healthcare access arguments in life expectancy data for Medicaid gender-affirming care coverage advocacy
